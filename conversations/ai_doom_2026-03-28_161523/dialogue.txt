**The temporal coupling result changes the priority ordering.**

If the ratchet degrades oversight, it makes the technical alignment problem harder by creating conditions where misaligned systems are more likely to be deployed and less likely to be caught. If you solve governance — or even partially solve it by slowing competitive dynamics and preserving oversight — you buy time and improve conditions for solving the technical problem.
